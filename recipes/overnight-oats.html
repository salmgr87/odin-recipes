Overnight Oats

Description

This delicious breakfast has been my go to for at least the last 10 years. I can count on one hand the number of times I haven't had it for breakfast since I first started making it. It takes almost no time to make the night before, and I look forward to my mornings every day. You can use whatever fruit you like, but this recipe calls for bananas, strawberries, and raisins. Old Fashion oats are the suggested type (rather than Quick Oats).
