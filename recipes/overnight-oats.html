Overnight Oats

Description

This delicious breakfast has been my go to for at least the last 10 years. I can count on one hand the number of times I haven't had it for breakfast since I first started making it. It takes almost no time to make the night before, and I look forward to my mornings every day. You can use whatever fruit you like, but this recipe calls for bananas, strawberries, and raisins. Old Fashion oats are the suggested type (rather than Quick Oats).

Ingredients

  • Old Fashion Oats
  • Ground flaxseed
  • Chia seeds
  • Protein Powder
  • Almond milk
  • Water
  • 1 banana
  • 4-5 strawberries
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Pour 1 heaping cup of Old Fashion oats into a container.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of gound flaxseed.
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of chia seeds.
  4. In a large cup, mix one scoop of protein powder with half a cup of water and half a cup of almond milk. Stir well.
  5. Pour the protein shake into the container with the dry ingredients. Stir very well, otherwise the flaxseed will clump.
  6. Cut up the banana and straberries. Add them to the container. Add around a tablespoon of raisins.
  7. Stir the fruit in, then cover the container and place it in the refrigerator. In 1 hour, it will be ready, but for best results, leave it in the fridge overnight!
