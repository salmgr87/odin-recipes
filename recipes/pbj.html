Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

The classic peanute butter and jelly sandwich! Yet another food I eat once a day 360 out of 365 days in a year. Don't overlook this simple staple. It's got enough energy to take you from noon until night. I recommend whole wheat bread, but choose your edible container!

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat bread
  • Trader Joe's Natural Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (any flavor you like!)
