Bean Dish

Description

Beans, beans are good for the... anyway! One of my all time favorite and simple recipes. Combine this with some rice, and you have a super healthy meal ready to go!

Ingredients

  • Black beans
  • Red kidney beans
  • Baked beans
  • Peas
  • Salt, pepper, and any spices you like

Steps

  1. If desired, add olive oil to a pan.
  2. If using cans, rinse the black beans, red kidney beans, and peas first.
  3. Add all beans and peas to the pan. Cook on low for 1-2 hours. Stir regularly.
  4. Add salt, pepper, and spices to taste.
